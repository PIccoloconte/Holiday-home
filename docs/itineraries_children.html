  • Stay preferences (check-in date, check-out date, special requests)

2. Use of Data

Your personal data will be used for:

  1. Managing bookings and providing vacation rental services
  2. Communicating with you to confirm bookings or respond to inquiries

  4. Improving our website and services

3. Data Sharing

We will not share your personal data with third parties, except in the following cases:

  • When necessary to complete a transaction or provide a requested service
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • With third-party service providers (e.g., payment systems) acting on our behalf

4. Data Security

We implement technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access or disclosure.

5. User Rights

You have the right to:

  1. Access your personal data
  2. Request correction or deletion of your data
  3. Object to the use of your data for promotional purposes
  4. Request the portability of your data to another service provider

6. Data Retention

We will retain your personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected or to comply with legal obligations.
